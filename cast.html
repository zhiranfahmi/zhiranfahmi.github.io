Walter White

Walter Hartwell White Sr., also known by his clandestine alias Heisenberg, He is a chemistry teacher that learns he has cancer and becomes a meth manufacturer.

Jesse Pinkman

Aaron Paul

Jesse Pinkman is a crystal meth cook and dealer, and works with his former high school chemistry teacher, Walter White in a meth operation. Jesse is the only character besides Walt to appear in every episode of the show.

Skyler White

Anna Gunn

Skyler White is Walte's wife, a mother of two and co-owner of A1A Car Wash.

Hank Schrader

Dean Norris

the brother-in-law of main character Walter White, and is a Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) agent in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Throughout the series, he leads the investigation of the methamphetamine cook "Heisenberg"

Walter White, Jr.

RJ Mitte

is the son of main protagonist Walter White and his wife Skyler. He has cerebral palsy, as manifested in speech difficulties and impaired motor control, for which he uses crutches.

Saul Goodman

Bob Odenkirk

Saul Goodman is Walt and Jesse's attorney. He's the quintessential shady lawyer.

Marie Schrader

Betsy Brandt

Marie is Skyler's sister and the wife of DEA agent Hank Schrader.

Mike Ehrmantraut

Jonathan Banks

Mike Ehrmantraut is a former Philadelphia police officer who works for Gus Fring—and, on occasion, Saul Goodman—as a private investigator, head of security, cleaner, and hitman.

Gustavo Fring

Giancarlo Esposito

Gustavo Fring is a major methamphetamine distributor in the Southwestern United States who uses several legitimate businesses, including a chain of successful fast food fried chicken restaurants called Los Pollos Hermanos (The Chicken Brothers) and an industrial laundry facility called Lavanderia Brillante, as fronts for a vast drug operation.

Jane Margolis

Krysten Ritter

Jane Margolis was a tattoo artist and Jesse Pinkman's neighbor, landlord and girlfriend. She was also a recovering drug addict and the daughter of Donald Margolis, an air-traffic controller.

Todd Alquist

Jesse Plemons

The youngest and freshest-faced member of the Vamonos Pest team, Todd is quiet and hard-working. He makes an early impression on Walt when he alerts him to a nanny-cam in a house that Walt and Jesse plan to cook meth in.

Lydia Rodarte-Quayle

Laura Fraser

Lydia Rodarte-Quayle was the Head of Logistics at Madrigal Electromotive GmbH, and was based at their office in Houston, Texas. She supplied methylamine to Gustavo Fring prior to the collapse of Gus' Drug Empire, and later aligned herself with Walt's Drug Empire and eventually with Jack Welker's Gang.
